Intercom Youth Foundation - Kenya

Home

Intercom Youth Foundation was founded in 2017 to do 3 types of missions in Kenya.

Medical Mission of Compassion

IYF works to identify needy areas in Kenya lacking in medical facilities and organized a 10 days Medical Mission of Compassion. Local qualified doctors are employed to provide completely free medical services to all residents. We have conducted this mission in 2017, 2018 and 2019. Medical Mission of Compassion couldn't be conductced during 2020 and 2021, but we are planning to conduct during July 2022 in Nyakach, Nyanza Province, Kenya.

Free Computer Skill Training

A lot of High School Graduates in Kenya and unable to afford high tution rates for a college degree. IYF identifes deserving students and conducts a free computer skill training for them conducted by church members from International Community Church, Apex, NC, USA. The instructors are professionals employed in the IT field by various companies. They volunteer their time and use custom developed training materials to deliver world class job oriented skills to the trainees. Training in conducted in Kisumu, Kenya during the month of July.

Nyanza Pastors Bible Conference

A lot of Pastors in Kenya would like to learn more about he Bible and serve their congregrations better. IYF organizes Nyanza Pastors Bible Conference and provides a day of in-depth training from the Bible by Pastor Joseph B. Jesudaon.
